The studio

Who we are.

Dead Format Games is an independent studio with a soft spot for the machines and genres the industry left behind — cartridge-era strike games, cassette mixtapes, and action movies that play it 90% straight. PERSIAN STRIKE: RETRIBUTION is our love letter to all three: a Desert Strike–style rotary-wing war, rebuilt with a living battlefield and a base you raise block by block. It is built in the Godot Engine and it ships when it's done.
