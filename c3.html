Autumn — the Caspian front

WORLD WAR

The reformed USSR and Pyongyang pick their side, and the lone-rebel war becomes something bigger. Fight the relay war under a changing northern sky — copper Hyrcanian forest, fog seas in the valleys, sleet climbing to the Alborz snowline. Rescue the downed drone teams and earn five friendly birds on your wing; hunt the armored train — eight sections of rolling siege artillery — down the coastal rails; and hold the stronghold, because it can fall while you're away. Then the snow starts, and doesn't stop for three winters.
